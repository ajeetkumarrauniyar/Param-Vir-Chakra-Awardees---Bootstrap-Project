Param Vir Chakra AWARDEES!

The Param Vir Chakra is the highest military decoration awarded for acts of valor in the Indian armed forces.
Established in 1950, it is presented to members of the Indian Army, Navy, and Air Force for their extraordinary bravery and selfless sacrifices in the line of duty.

Param Vir Chakra
